# Privacy Policy

**Crypto Culture Music**

**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Crypto Culture Music handles information in connection with its internal digital media application.

Crypto Culture Music is an internal tool for content distribution and media operations. It is not designed to collect personal data from external users, public viewers, listeners, subscribers, or social media audiences.

## 2. Internal Tool Statement

Crypto Culture Music is used internally to manage proprietary digital media content, including audio, video, artwork, metadata, descriptions, and related publishing assets.

The application is not a public consumer service and does not provide public user accounts, public profiles, social networking functionality, or external audience data collection.

## 3. No External User Data Collection

Crypto Culture Music does not collect external user data.

Specifically, Crypto Culture Music does not intentionally collect:

* Names of external viewers or listeners
* Email addresses of external viewers or listeners
* Phone numbers
* Home addresses
* Payment information
* Social media audience profiles
* Public follower data
* Public listener data
* Behavioral tracking data from external users
* Cookies for external user tracking
* Sensitive personal information

Any audience analytics available through third-party platforms are governed by those platforms’ own privacy policies and terms.

## 4. Internal Operational Data

Crypto Culture Music may process limited internal operational information necessary to run the application, such as:

* Authorized user login details
* Internal account identifiers
* Access timestamps
* System logs
* Uploaded file names
* Publishing metadata
* Workflow activity
* Error logs
* Security logs

This information is used only for internal administration, security, troubleshooting, publishing workflows, and content distribution operations.

## 5. Uploaded Media

All uploaded media is proprietary.

Uploaded media may include:

* Audio recordings
* Music tracks
* Video clips
* Cover artwork
* Promotional graphics
* Captions
* Metadata
* Brand assets
* Educational scripts
* Distribution materials

Crypto Culture Music does not treat uploaded media as public user-generated content. Uploaded media is controlled, owned, or licensed by Crypto Culture Music or its authorized rights holders.

## 6. Use of Information

Crypto Culture Music uses internal operational information only to:

* Operate the application
* Manage media files
* Prepare content for distribution
* Support internal publishing workflows
* Maintain security
* Troubleshoot technical issues
* Prevent unauthorized access
* Comply with applicable legal obligations

Crypto Culture Music does not sell, rent, trade, or monetize personal data.

## 7. Third-Party Platforms

Crypto Culture Music may support distribution or publishing workflows involving external platforms, such as:

* Music streaming platforms
* Social media platforms
* Video platforms
* Website hosting services
* Analytics dashboards
* Cloud storage providers
* Automation tools

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Crypto Culture Music is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

Crypto Culture Music does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect data from external public users.

Internal system cookies or authentication tokens may be used only for authorized access, session management, security, or application functionality.

## 9. Data Sharing

Crypto Culture Music does not share external user data because it does not collect external user data.

Internal operational data or proprietary media may be shared only with authorized service providers, platforms, contractors, or collaborators when necessary for:

* Hosting
* Storage
* Publishing
* Distribution
* Automation
* Security
* Technical support
* Legal compliance

## 10. Data Security

Crypto Culture Music uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect internal operational data and proprietary media from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Data Retention

Crypto Culture Music retains internal operational data and proprietary media for as long as necessary to support business, publishing, operational, legal, or security purposes.

Media files may be retained indefinitely unless deleted by authorized personnel.

## 12. Access and Control

Because Crypto Culture Music is an internal tool and does not collect external user data, external public users generally do not have personal data access, deletion, correction, or portability rights within the application.

Authorized internal users may request access, correction, or deletion of their internal account information by contacting the application administrator.

## 13. Children’s Privacy

Crypto Culture Music is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 14. International Use

Crypto Culture Music may use service providers or platforms located in different jurisdictions. Internal operational data and proprietary media may be processed where those providers operate.

## 15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Crypto Culture Music may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be effective when posted or made available internally.
